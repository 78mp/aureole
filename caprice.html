Shortened lore

The one who catches.

Manic gremlin human-fisher. His followers are the fish, entranced by the rewards of human flesh. He was originally imagined in Headspace to be a righteous revolutionary for the freedom, struggling for the freedom of the labrats and capturing the hearts of the suffering. But-

Out of obscure emotions, he devolved into a corrupt populist and a sadomasochist, with his human followers as fish. Now he lives in a realm of reticence and loneliness. No human being dares to come near him for fear of the harpoon.
