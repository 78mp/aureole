A legitimate graduate from The Grounds. He now overworks as a medical intern.

privileged . unserious . exhausted

Traits. sociable, polite, friendly outside; an aloof tactician inside.
He likes to distance others from himself emotionally to not get any moral blackmail. Nonetheless, he is very compassionate towards other humans.

Ideology. moral nihilism.
At a very young age, he was educated to view everyone else as rivals to be defeated or peers to be exploited. Vice versa, he thought of others to be treating him the same way.
He despises that mindset.

Bonds. The childhood friends that he has lost. The impermanence of them makes him scared to form any more close relationships and distrustful of the sincerity of other humans.

「 Act I 」

AAH skip the boring lore

Born from a wealthy, privileged household, Kace was treated differently to everyone else. He had many friends but no close ones, except for a single childhood one who drifted apart in teenage years.


His parents loved him, but perhaps a little too much. They bled into his life and stained every corner with red. His belongings were red, the food were red, his mannerisms were red, even his speech was red. He was ashamed of those blotches and hurt at their prevalence. He tried to wash them off, but they always reappear, as obdurate as a human's flesh. One day, he swallowed red. He was borderline traumatised. He knew ingesting equates to assimilation, and then he wouldn't tell red from black and white. Neither would the others, his friends and his family.
